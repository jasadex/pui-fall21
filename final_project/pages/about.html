Our Mission

Promoting stable long-term same-sex relationships in young LGBT communities

This project aims to highlight the similarity and differences between same-sex and different-sex relationships, and promote stable long-term relationships among gay male couples. We break down relationship stages to show the similarity between same-sex and different-sex couples, while highlighting the unique challenges young LGBT couples could face in each stage. We also discuss factors that could potentially be affecting the relationship stability in young LGBT communities based on research. The project hopes to educate the unique needs of young LGBT relationships, while also showing that there are many normative experiences that young LGBTs share with their heterosexual counterparts -- your concerns in relationships may not always reflect your sexual and gender identities!
